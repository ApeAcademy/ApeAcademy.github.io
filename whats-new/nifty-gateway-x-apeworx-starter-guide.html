Ape is excited to share ApeStarter.fun, a website built by our community contributor 0xKofi. He autonomously deployed and created super easy tutorial videos about Ape’s data on-chain capabilities. 

‍

Not Ape sponsored, but definitely Ape approved! 

‍

Kofi is the founder of Nifty Gateway, and he's been an excellent member of our community of data scientists since we launched our Discord earlier this year. Over the last few months, he has developed many incredible data tools available to everyone—including this year's ape-arbitrum plugin. Now he's taking things one step further by creating a unique Ape Framework starter guide for data junkies looking to get their hands dirty with on-chain analytics! You don't need any previous programming experience or local installations to follow along! You can use ApeFramework to read on-chain stats, build bots and execute transactions that will give you the upper hand in any NFT or DeFi strategy. 

‍

You'll need to download a Google Collab notebook and understand basic Web3 infrastructure knowledge to get started. It's easy enough for anyone who has never coded before—even if they don't know their way around a virtual environment! And if you have some coding experience already, it's even more fun because it gives you more control over what your  dApp does when it has access to your smart contracts.

‍

Whether you're looking to get started with blockchain development or want more control over how your dApps interact with smart contracts and other blockchains like Ethereum, Ape is your go-to "swiss army knife." Ape Framework is a community-driven software that allows users to build and explore Web3 together. We are excited to support our creator community by providing them with software to do anything they want. As we like to say, if it doesn't exist, you can make it with Ape!
